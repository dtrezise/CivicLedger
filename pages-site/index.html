Interpretation boundaries

Methodology

What the visualization can establish, and what it deliberately does not claim.

Reported activity

Transactions reflect disclosed ranges and dates. A midpoint may size a marker but is never presented as an exact amount.

Service time

Career mode counts active federal service only. Calendar mode preserves gaps between nonconsecutive roles.

Event evidence

Relationship tiers describe source-backed institutional, jurisdictional, asset, or sector context. They do not establish causation.

Record states

Fixture, parser preview, reviewed, and production records remain distinct throughout the interface and exported data.
