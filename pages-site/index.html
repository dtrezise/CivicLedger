Methodology

What This Site Does and Does Not Claim

Neutral Presentation

CivicLedger presents filing dates, reported ranges, disclosure lag, source context, and parser provenance without ranking or accusing officials.

Fixture Data

This Pages edition uses generated demo records. It is public-facing as a product preview, not a production public disclosure database.

Source Separation

House, Senate, OGE, and judiciary records remain branch-aware because their forms, access workflows, and disclosure rules differ.

Provenance First

The full app stores raw documents before parser output and requires human review before promoting normalized filings or trades.
